About ArchiFlask

Built by people who have actually run the projects.

ArchiFlask is the operating platform for design, construction & PMC firms — born from two friends who lived the chaos of running real projects, and set out to fix it with a system.

Our story

A friendship that goes back to childhood.

One of us built software; the other designed and delivered residential villas. After collaborating on several projects — including our own homes and family developments — we ran straight into the same wall over and over: managing communication, approvals, site updates, documents, and coordination.

The turning point was realising these frustrations weren't ours alone. They were everywhere in architecture and construction. So we set out to solve them with technology.

That conviction became ArchiFlask — a platform built to bring clarity, collaboration, and control to every project.

The product

One platform for the whole firm.

ArchiFlask brings projects, clients, teams, drawings, site activity, approvals and profitability into one place — so a growing firm runs on a system instead of memory. It's the Standard Operating Procedure for design and construction practices.

Clarity

Every drawing, revision and decision in one trail. The whole firm sees the same picture.

Collaboration

Teams, clients and the site stay in sync — on web and mobile, with their own access.

₹

Control

Effort, deadlines and profitability tracked to the hour and the rupee — per project, per person.
